Project Overview

Revolutionary Cardiovascular Risk Prediction

CVD Predictor is a state-of-the-art machine learning application that delivers 90.29% accuracy in cardiovascular disease risk assessment.

Our system combines real-world cardiovascular data from multiple clinical sources with advanced machine learning algorithms to provide instant, personalized risk assessments. The model has been trained on 800K+ real data points from 5 different cardiovascular datasets, ensuring robust and reliable predictions.

Core Algorithm

Our system uses a Random Forest Classifier that has been extensively trained and validated on real cardiovascular data.

  • Feature Engineering: 11 clinical risk factors
  • Cross-Validation: 10-fold validation for reliability

Real Data Foundation

Primary Training Datasets

Our model is trained on 5 real cardiovascular datasets totaling over 800,000 clinical samples:

  • Cardio Train Dataset: 70,000 cardiovascular health records
  • Heart Disease Dataset: 319,000 US cardiovascular cases
  • CVD Cleaned Dataset: 308,000 processed cardiovascular records
  • Cardiovascular Risk Dataset: 308,000 risk factor analyses
  • Synthetic Enhancement: 50,000 AI-generated samples for balance
Data Quality & Features

Each dataset provides comprehensive cardiovascular health information:

  • Demographics: Age, gender, ethnicity
  • Physical Metrics: BMI, height, weight
  • Vital Signs: Blood pressure, heart rate
  • Biochemical: Cholesterol, glucose levels
  • Lifestyle: Smoking, alcohol, physical activity
